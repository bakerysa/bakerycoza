Bakery is a digital agency specialising in Product Strategy, UX/UI Design and Development.

We're a full-service development studio specialising in user experience design, web/app development, and technology solutions. Together with our partner agency, The Bread, we offer turnkey solutions to companies, brands, and individuals.

Product Strategy

We work with our clients to define what their product is, what it does, and how it works. We pair innovation with user experience in the best way possible.

User Experience

For digital products and services to resonate with audiences, we ensure UX design informs all decisions made from landing to leaving.

User Research

Helping you understand your audience and their behaviors through research and testing. This knowledge informs every step of our process.

Interaction Design

Understanding the human element in a tech product allows us to ensure interaction is the first consideration in developing our digital products.

Web Development

We obsess over code. This is what allows us to promise website and web apps that look beautiful, communicate brilliantly, and run quietly under the hood.

E-Commerce

We help you and your business sell your products or services online. Ensuring functionality, security, and ease-of-use are the things we obsess over so you don't have to.

Brand Identity

Together with The Bread we develop brand identities, visual languages, and graphic elements that captivate and delight customers.

Mobile Apps

Mobile is more important than ever and we're here to help you take advantage of the most important development of our age since the onset of the internet.

Prototyping

We take the guess-work out of digital and ensure all products, no matter how big or small, are thoroughly prototyped and tested up until final deployment.

Speaking in Code

Speaking in Code is a new event hosted regularly for developers to meet up, share ideas and learn from one another.

Each event features a speaker covering a topic of interest relating to development, experience design, product strategy or technology as a whole.
